# Oliver & Company (1988)

![](https://media.trakt.tv/images/movies/000/007/135/fanarts/thumb/aca54a077a.jpg)Putting its own 'twist' on the story of Oliver Twist, the orange runt of a litter of kittens must fight for survival on the rough streets on New York City, finding unlikely friends in the dogs owned by a down-on-his luck man named Fagin. Soon, Oliver and his new band of comrades must fight for survival when Fagin is unable to pay his debts.
